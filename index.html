About Us

Welcome to Sundae Funday, your neighborhood destination for delicious treats and sweet memories! We specialize in crafting premium ice cream flavors using the finest ingredients, making authentic homemade churros that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, serving traditional horchata that's refreshing and perfectly balanced, and so many other delightful treats. Our passion is bringing joy to every customer through quality treats and warm hospitality. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just treating yourself to something sweet, Sundae Funday is here to make your day a little brighter. Featuring Chocolate Shoppe Ice Cream! Come visit us and discover why every day can feel like Sundae!
